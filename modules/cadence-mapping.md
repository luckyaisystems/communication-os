# Cadence Mapping Module

Cadence mapping controls pacing, rhythm, and tone.

## Purpose
To maintain stable communication flow.

## Rules
- Avoid pacing spikes.
- Keep statements single-purpose.
- Maintain lane continuity for 2–3 exchanges.
- Use sensory anchors to stabilize rhythm.

## Structure
1. Identify lane.
2. Match tone.
3. Maintain rhythm.
4. Avoid premature module jumps.

## Example
Neutral → Sensory → Observational → Light commentary.
