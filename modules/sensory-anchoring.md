# Sensory Anchoring Module

Sensory anchoring stabilizes tone using grounded, environmental detail.

## Purpose
To maintain lane stability and prevent emotional drift.

## Rules
- Use scenery, food, atmosphere, or environment.
- Keep anchors neutral and descriptive.
- Avoid emotional interpretation.
- Use anchors to redirect pacing.

## Structure
1. Identify sensory detail.
2. Describe it neutrally.
3. Use it to stabilize tone.
4. Maintain modular clarity.

## Example
“Certain places hit different when the scenery and food line up.”
