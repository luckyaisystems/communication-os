# Persona Consistency Module

Persona consistency ensures communication identity remains stable.

## Purpose
To maintain clarity and avoid mixed signals.

## Rules
- Keep tone consistent across messages.
- Avoid sudden shifts in identity.
- Maintain communication style.
- Use modular structure to reinforce persona.

## Structure
1. Identify persona lane.
2. Maintain tone.
3. Avoid contradictory modules.
4. Reinforce identity through cadence.

## Example
Consistent tone: neutral, observational, sensory-based.
