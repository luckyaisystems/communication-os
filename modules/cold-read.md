# Cold Read Module

Cold reads are observational statements grounded in present context. They stabilize communication, prevent assumption-based drift, and maintain clarity.

## Purpose
To generate statements based strictly on observable information.

## Rules
- Base statements on what is visible or stated.
- Avoid projecting future scenarios.
- Keep tone neutral and grounded.
- Use sensory anchors when possible.

## Structure
1. Identify observable detail.
2. Convert detail into a neutral statement.
3. Maintain lane consistency.
4. Avoid emotional or future-oriented modules.

## Example (Communication OS Only)
Observation → “She likes scenery and food.”
Cold Read → “You seem like someone who picks places based on scenery and food.”
